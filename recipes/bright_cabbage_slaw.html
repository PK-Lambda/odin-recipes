Ingredients

  • 1/2 medium head of red or green cabbage
  • 1/2 small red onion, thinly alixws
  • 55ml lemon juice
  • Salt
  • 15g coarsely chopped parsley leaves
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 6 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Quarter the cabbage through the core. Use a sharp knife to cut the core out at an angle.
  2. Season with two generous pinches of salt to help draw out water, toss the slices, and set aside.
  3. In a small bowl, toss the slices onion with the lemon juice and let it sit for 20 minutes to macerate.
  4. After 20 minutes, drain any water the cabbage may have given off.
  5. Dress the slaw with the vinegar and olive oil. Toss very well to combine
  6. Tase and adjust, adding the remaining macerating lemon juice and salt as needed.
  7. When your palate zings with pleasure, it's ready. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
  8. Enjoy!
